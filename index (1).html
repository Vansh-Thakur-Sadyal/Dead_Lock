System Learning (RLFF)
Each feedback action updates faculty ranking weights in real-time through Reinforcement Learning from Faculty Feedback.
How It Works
"Faculty mark queries as relevant or irrelevant. Relevant feedback increases their domain score by +0.03×(1−score). Irrelevant decreases it by −0.07×score."
Relevant: score += 0.03 × (1 − score)
Irrelevant: score -= 0.07 × score
Final: 0.55×semantic + 0.45×domain
Escalation Logic
Queries are automatically escalated when they exceed the response timeout or faculty marks them irrelevant.
Step 1 — Initial Routing
Query assigned to top-ranked faculty
Step 2 — Rerouting
If irrelevant or timeout → next faculty assigned
Step 3 — Escalation
After 2 failed attempts → escalation_level incremented
